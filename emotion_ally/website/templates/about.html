About Emotion Ally

The Problem: LGBT+ youth are facing a mental health epidemic. Data from the Trevor Project show an upward trend in negative mental health symptoms.

Our Solution: Using natural language processing technology, Emotion Ally can assist LGBT+ youth in learning emotion identification and management and connect them to the appropriate resources, without any judgement.

By understanding their emotions better, LGBT+ youth can better manage their symptoms, get support, and thrive like their peers.
